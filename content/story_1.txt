Title: Amazing Animal Facts

Did you know? Honey never spoils. Archaeologists found 3000-year-old honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that's still perfectly edible today!

Your body contains approximately 37 trillion cells, all working together in perfect harmony.

A group of flamingos is called a flamboyance, and they're born with grey feathers, not the pink color we see them now.

Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.

Hummingbirds are the only birds that can fly backwards, and their wings beat about 80 times per second.

Sharks have been around for over 450 million years, making them older than dinosaurs!
