Title: Incredible Human Body Facts

Your nose can remember up to 50,000 different scents, making it one of your most powerful senses.

The human brain uses 20 percent of your body's energy, even though it only weighs about 2 percent of your body weight.

Your heart beats about 100,000 times a day, pumping roughly 2,000 gallons of blood through your body.

You shed about 30,000 to 40,000 dead skin cells every minute. That's about 1.5 pounds of skin per year!

Your bones are stronger than steel, pound for pound. A block of bone the size of a matchbox can support nine tons of weight!

Your fingernails grow faster than your toenails. They grow about 0.1 millimeters per day.

Your tongue has about 10,000 taste buds, and they all regenerate completely about every two weeks.

If you laid all your blood vessels end to end, they would stretch about 100,000 kilometers around the Earth!
