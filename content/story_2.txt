Title: Mind-Blowing Space Facts
The Sun's core is so hot that it reaches temperatures of 27 million degrees Fahrenheit!
A day on Venus is longer than its year. Venus takes 243 Earth days to rotate once, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.
There are more stars in the universe than there are grains of sand on all of Earth's beaches combined.
Neptune has the fastest winds in our solar system, reaching speeds of 1,200 miles per hour!
One million Earths could fit inside the Sun. It's truly a massive ball of burning gas.
Lightning on Jupiter is about 1,000 times more powerful than lightning on Earth.
If you could somehow stand on the surface of a neutron star, you would be instantly crushed due to the immense gravitational pull.
